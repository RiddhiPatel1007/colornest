ColorNest is an open collection of beautiful color palettes, created by Riddhi Patel. ColorNest started as a personal small project built to share trendy color. ColorNest was created with the goal of celebrating the beauty of colors, and to serve as a go-to resource for color inspiration.

Who creates the color palettes?

You, the users, are the ones who create the palettes using ColorNest's palette creator. The collection is open, and everyone can create and submit their own color combination.

Made by Riddhi Patel

ColorNest was designed and developed by Riddhi Patel, a passionate Front-End Developer dedicated to creating beautiful and useful web experiences.
